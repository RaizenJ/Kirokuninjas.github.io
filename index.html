INTRODUCTION

In the beginning of the rise of streaming content, There were 6 flawless titans. Michelle the great, Princess the impressive, Patricia the fabulous, Raizen the outstanding, Rodwayne the magnificent and Emy the adorable. All these beasts of streamers will combine together in one legendary stream, All their values and focus will be laid upon a single game wherein they will educate us with tricks, Spots, Strats and everything in between. Call of Duty Mobile will be absolutely dominated on the day the stream launches and all the audience can do is brace for greatness.

WHAT ARE WE DOING ON THE STREAM?

We plan on showing our pre-recorded gameplay of Call of Duty: Mobile and reacting to it altogether. We can also give tips and tricks during the stream so the audience can have an idea of how we play CODM. During the stream, we can add a little chit chat session just to get to know each other more. Since most of us don't really play the game well, we can just laugh at our mistakes and entertain our audience. Also, we can ask for tips from our audience which is a good way to interact with the viewers. As a group, we look forward to watching each other's gameplay and to hopefully learn something new from each other.

WHY ARE WE DOING THE STREAM?

We only aim to have fun and have a good interaction with our audience. Besides having fun, we also, together with our audience, want to improve in playing the game, establishing teamwork within the players to be a good and powerful team. As an online group, we would like to try anything to create a great bonding experience for us to remember and to share. We look forward to the funny and eventful moments during the live steam.
